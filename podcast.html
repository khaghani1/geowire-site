How We Make This

AI-assisted intelligence briefing — powered by Google NotebookLM
🤖
Powered by Google NotebookLM
GeoWire data feeds into NotebookLM to generate natural-sounding, fact-grounded intelligence audio.
1

Live data ingestion

FRED economic data, GDELT conflict events, EIA oil stats, and GeoWire's own scoring models are pulled fresh before each episode production cycle.

2

Intelligence synthesis

GeoWire's editorial framework organizes raw data into narrative arcs: the conflict thread, the economic thread, the geopolitical thread. Each episode follows a structured briefing format.

3

NotebookLM audio generation

Google NotebookLM's "Audio Overview" feature synthesizes a two-host dialogue from the curated source documents. The result sounds like two analysts discussing the brief — not a robot reading text.

4

Editorial review

Every episode is reviewed for factual accuracy against GeoWire's confidence tiers before publishing. Disputed claims are either removed or clearly labeled.

5

Multi-platform distribution

Episodes publish simultaneously on Spotify, Apple Podcasts, and via RSS. The player is also embedded directly on this page for frictionless listening.
