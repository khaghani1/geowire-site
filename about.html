About GeoWire

GeoWire is an independent recession intelligence platform. We track how geopolitical shocks, energy crises, inflation, rates, labor, and supply chains change recession probability — before consensus catches up.

The 10-Factor Recession Model

GeoWire's recession probability score is derived from a weighted composite of 10 economic and geopolitical factors. Each factor is scored 0–100 (where higher = more recessionary pressure), then combined using evidence-weighted coefficients calibrated against historical recession onset patterns.

Data Sources

GeoWire pulls from the following public and commercial data sources:

  • FRED — Federal Reserve Economic Data (St. Louis Fed). GDP, unemployment, yield curves, CPI, housing starts.
  • BLS — Bureau of Labor Statistics. Non-farm payrolls, initial claims, JOLTS, PPI.
  • EIA — US Energy Information Administration. Oil/gas production, inventories, price forecasts.
  • CoinGecko — Crypto market data. Bitcoin, Ethereum as risk-sentiment indicators.
  • Kalshi — Prediction markets. Probability-implied forecasts for economic events.
  • Polymarket — Decentralized prediction markets. Independent market-implied recession odds.
  • GDELT — Global Database of Events, Language, and Tone. Conflict tracking and geopolitical sentiment.
  • Bloomberg / CME / ICE — Commodity and financial market prices (via public redistributors).

Editorial Principles

NOT FINANCIAL ADVICE
GeoWire provides intelligence and probability analysis, not investment recommendations. All content is for informational purposes only. Always consult a qualified financial advisor before making investment decisions.
CONFIDENCE RATINGS ON ALL OUTPUTS
Every data point and analysis carries a confidence badge — Confirmed, Multi-source, Single-source, Model-inferred, or Disputed. We never present uncertain data as fact.
AI SUMMARIES LABELED
Where AI-generated summaries or model inferences are used, they are clearly labeled as such. Human editorial review is applied to all published analysis.
INDEPENDENT & AD-FREE
GeoWire is independently operated with no advertiser relationships that could influence editorial decisions. We are supported by readers.
